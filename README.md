# Indian Tech Job Market Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes **23,201+ Indian technology job postings** to uncover hiring trends, salary patterns, work modes, and skill demand in the Indian tech industry. The analysis was performed using **Python, SQL, and Excel**, with a focus on generating business insights that can help job seekers, recruiters, and hiring managers make informed decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze hiring trends across the Indian technology sector.
- Identify the most in-demand job roles and hiring locations.
- Compare remote, hybrid, and onsite job opportunities.
- Examine company ratings and salary distributions.
- Generate business insights using data visualization and SQL analysis.

# Business Insights

- Data Scientist and Data Analyst positions account for a significant share of job postings.
- Hiring is concentrated in major technology hubs across India.
- Hybrid and onsite roles are more common than fully remote positions.
- Company ratings are generally clustered in the mid-to-high range.
- Senior roles tend to offer higher salary ranges where salary information is disclosed.
- Salary transparency remains limited, with many employers not publishing salary details.
- Demand is concentrated in a few core technology skill domains.
- Fresher-friendly opportunities exist but represent a smaller proportion of total openings.
- A relatively small number of companies contribute a large share of total job postings.
